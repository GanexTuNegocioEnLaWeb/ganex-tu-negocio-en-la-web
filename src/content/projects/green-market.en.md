## Project Context

Consumer convenience and instant delivery have become central to online retail, especially for essential categories like groceries. Apps in this space must deliver:

- Fast browsing and checkout
- Clear visibility of products and offers
- Seamless order & delivery tracking
- Trustworthy user data policies

Green Market operates in a **competitive grocery delivery ecosystem**, where user experience, performance, and conversion rates are critical.

## UX/UI Observations

From a UX perspective, grocery delivery apps benefit from:

- Simple category navigation
- Quick access to current offers
- Minimal steps from selection to checkout
- Clear delivery and payment information

> Grocery apps must balance product volume with speed and clarity.

## Key Features (Inferred)

- Browse grocery categories
- Add products to cart
- Place order for delivery
- Weekly special offers
- In-app account & contact support

All of these features are expected in modern e-commerce grocery apps, though implementation quality varies per platform.
