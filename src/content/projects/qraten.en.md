## QRaten | Smart QR-Based Attendance Tracking

**QRaten** is a **mobile application and web platform** created to **automate attendance tracking** using **QR codes**.  
It is ideal for **educational institutions, companies, and organizations** that require a fast, accurate, and easy-to-use attendance management system.

> The main goal: **replace manual attendance processes with an instant, error-free digital solution**.

## How QRaten Works

The workflow is simple and efficient:

1. An administrator generates a **unique QR code**
2. Users scan the QR code with their mobile devices
3. Attendance is recorded instantly
4. Data is stored and available in real time

> No paper lists  
> No signatures  
> No friction
