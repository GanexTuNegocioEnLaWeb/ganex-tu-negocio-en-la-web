## UX/UI Analysis

From a user experience perspective, the platform focuses on:

* Professional and sober visual identity
* Structured content hierarchy
* Informational clarity over aggressive marketing
* Desktop-first experience with responsive adaptation

> In healthcare, **clarity beats creativity**.

## Content & Information Architecture

Key content pillars identified:

* Company background and expertise
* Medical products and solutions
* Industry applications
* Contact and institutional trust signals

A clear **information hierarchy** reduces friction and improves comprehension.
